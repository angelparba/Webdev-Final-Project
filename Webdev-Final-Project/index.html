SHEILA BARORO

(PROUNOUNS: SHE/HER)
CPA, CGA, PMP

Sheila is a finance expert and a passionate leader serving not-for-profit organizations. She has extensive experience in Canada's not-to-profit and government sectors. Her recent role wa the Director of Finance and Administration of Elizabeth Fry Toronto managing Finance., HR, Facilities, IT and Administration. She had also worked with The Canadian Partnership Against Canser, a pan-Canadian organization focused on Financial and Corporate Planning and providing business support to partners across the country. She had previous accounting and finance roles in Public Health Ontario (PHO) and other not not-for-profit and profit sectors in Canada and the Philippines.

Sheila migrated from the Philippines to Toronto over 15 years ago. Her roots and values had kept her grounded as a leader. Sheila is fun to work with and very efficient in what she does. As a person of colour, Sheila is a big proponent of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion (DEI).
